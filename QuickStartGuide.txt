

1. Install "MCRInstaller.exe"

2. Launch "PhasorNet_train.exe"

3. Choose the training file. An example is shown in "TrainingSamples.txt"

4. Enter the training parameters.
Length of learner: number of training samples.
Temporal resolution (ns): bin of the histogram in nanosecond.

5. A network file is saved in "PhasorNetX.mat"
where X is a number: 0, 1, 2...
